Call For Proposals (CFP)
- Community Builders' World 2022

Community Builders' World Conference will be an interactive hybrid 1-day conference with keynote presentations, instructor-led workshops, expert panel discussions, one on one interviews, live entertainment, networking after-party. Community builders will have a wide range of opportunities to educate themselves on important topics in the community world, get inspired about how to build and nurture a great community and make some meaningful connections.

Important Dates

  • Community Builders' World Conference will be held in Prague in 2022 on 10th June 2022.
  • Abstract Submission Deadline: Sunday, April 17th, 2022 at 11 pm CET.
  • Speaker Notification of Acceptance: Sunday, April 30th, 2022 at 11 pm CET.

Should I submit my idea?

Yes, definitely! Submitting proposals is your job. Worrying about what fits the program is ours. When in doubt, submit it. To aid both new and experienced speakers, we offer support to help you develop your unfinished ideas into a great session.

By submitting, you agree:

  • to the Code of Conduct
  • that we can make photographs and video recordings of your sessions
  • that we can publish your information, photos, and videos

Topics

Our conference mission and vision are to tackle topics around trends, innovation, challenges, and the future of community management. We want to also help with the basics and share best practices. If you have a topic that doesn't cover those exact areas, but you still think it would be interesting for our audience - feel free to apply. We'd like our agenda to be diverse and educational.

Formats

Lightning Talks (5 min) - your talk can be a speech with supporting slides and may include a short demonstration.

Workshops (1-3 hours) we love practical sessions! An ideal workshop or hands-on session:

  • favors practice over theory
  • is focused on a methodology, or an approach, rather than a technology
  • can range from experimental to well-known formats
  • puts the 'work' in 'workshop': modeling, drawing, writing, discussing, …
  • encourages collaboration between participants (eg pairing or teamwork)
  • makes you want to go to the office on Monday and change the way you work!

Spoken language

All sessions are in English.

Submission Process

  • Send us as many proposals as you’d like, for as many as the accepted formats as you like, before the submission deadline on April 17, 2022.
  • Even before the deadline, we might start accepting proposals, so being early helps.
  • We might ask you to have a short conference call with us.
  • We’ll notify you at the latest 14 days after the submission deadline whether your idea is accepted or not. We'll ask for final confirmation before announcing you.
  • We'll put you in touch with our content team or other speakers, to tell you the next steps and details of the conference.

Increase Your Chances

Evaluating a submission based on nothing but an abstract is hard. Help us get to know you and your ideas by:

  • Sending us links to your content or community.
  • Blogging about your topic.
  • Talk about it at a local meetup.
  • Record your own video, ... Be creative!
  • Please be sure your submission focuses on community management and/or projects, not products specific to your company. We will not select any talks that look like product or vendor pitches.
  • Building a program involves lots of trade-offs, and often we have the painful task of refusing great proposals for other reasons than the topic. If yours is not accepted, please consider this an invitation to try again next year.

Your advantages as a speaker

To stand in front of a group of people as a speaker and pass on one's knowledge is a challenge - but also an award. Teaching something to others ultimately leads to even more knowledge about a topic (teach to learn). We want you to be a speaker at our conferences and show you here just only some advantages you have besides learning:

  • Free conference & afterparty visit
  • As a speaker, you will receive free access to the conference for the entire duration of the event. Balanced and varied catering at the conference is also included. You will receive your conference badge on-site.
  • Your own presentation platform
  • Present yourself with your lectures to a great community, share your knowledge and increase your profile.
  • Benefit from various networking opportunities, exchange ideas with other speakers, participants, and exhibitors, and make new contacts and business opportunities.

Review Process

All papers are committee reviewed by Community builders. Special consideration will be given to new presenters, underrepresented members of the community, and papers with social impact content. Our reviewing process and reviews are not open to the public.
Community Builders' World Conference is an inclusive hybrid event. Diversity and inclusion make our community strong. We encourage participation from the most varied and diverse backgrounds possible and want to be very clear about where we stand. We will not accept discrimination of any kind in the submitted content.

Technical Equipment

Especially in the case of full-day workshops, participants often need software to participate. They should install this software in advance so that the group can immediately get to grips with the topic. Therefore you should tell us about this software in advance.

What to think of:

‧ If the participants need certain software for your workshop, please let us know in advance. ‧ Please bring your own laptop with you, as we do not provide it. ‧ Think of an adapter if you want to work with an Apple product.

What we think of for you, the following equipment is available in the lecture rooms:
